We have been in Liberty Hill since 2004

At Comanche Trail Veterinary Center, the patient is our first priority. We are dedicated to serving our patients and see pet owners as a vital part of that process. We strive to spend the time necessary to make sure that pet owners receive the kind of guidance and knowledge they need to be best equipped to help their pets.

We are intentional about making ourselves available to our clients in order to better serve both their needs and the needs of their pets. In all aspects of our job we remember that pets are beloved and we treat both pets and their people with the kind of respect and compassion we wish for ourselves.
